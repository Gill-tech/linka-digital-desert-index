Digital desert typologies — 5 intervention archetypes

🟢 Urban Connected

High coverage + high use. Nairobi, Kiambu, Mombasa.

1–2 counties · DDI: 0–25
→ Sustain. Focus on quality, speeds, inclusivity for PWDs.

🔵 Peri-Urban Connected

High coverage, moderate use. Transitional counties near urban hubs.

3–4 counties · DDI: 15–35
→ Last-mile device financing, SME digital adoption programs.

🟠 Covered but Unaffordable

4G exists. Data cost exceeds 3.5% GNI/capita. Use suppressed.

~14 counties · DDI: 28–55
→ Zero-rating, shared plans, USF-subsidised access points.

🟣 Covered, Low Skills

Signal present. Literacy/education suppress adoption.

~15 counties · DDI: 40–65
→ Swahili digital literacy, community hubs, school programs.

🔴 Infrastructure Gap

Low coverage + low electricity. ASAL counties. Multi-barrier exclusion.

~15 counties · DDI: 60–100
→ USF towers, solar base stations, LEO satellite, REA co-invest.

⬜ The Wajir-Mandera Anomaly

Fast internet growth 2019→2024 despite ASAL geography. High cost burden remains.

2 counties · Fast growth, high DDI
→ Monitor. Diaspora-driven? M-Pesa adoption pathways?

Policy recommendations — linked to evidence
💰

Affordability interventions

For 14+ counties where cost exceeds 3.5% GNI/capita

  • Zero-rate education + government portals
  • Community shared WiFi (market centres)
  • Spectrum cost reduction → ISP pass-through
  • Safaricom Lipa Mdogo Mdogo device financing
  • Household data voucher subsidy program
📚

Digital skills programs

For 15+ counties with coverage but low education index

  • Swahili-first digital literacy curriculum
  • Sub-county digital hubs at existing schools
  • Train-the-trainer via local champions
  • WhatsApp-based micro-learning delivery
  • Women + youth targeted cohorts
📡

Infrastructure investment

For 15 critical counties with coverage + electricity gaps

  • USF deployment to arid/semi-arid counties
  • Solar-powered base stations (off-grid)
  • LEO satellite (Starlink-type) pilots
  • Tower co-location mandates to cut cost
  • Rural Electrification Authority co-investment
Data sources & methodology

Data sources used

ITU DataHub Internet use, 4G coverage, mobile broadband subscriptions
KNBS Housing Survey 2023/24 County-level internet use, mobile ownership, computer access
Communications Authority KE ICT access and usage disaggregated report 2024
World Bank WDI Electricity access, GNI per capita, poverty rates
GSMA MCI 2024 Affordability sub-index, mobile connectivity index
Kenya Census 2019 + KNBS Demographics, education attainment by county

DDI Construction

DDI = 0.25 × Coverage_Gap_norm
      + 0.30 × Affordability_Gap_norm
      + 0.25 × Electricity_Gap_norm
      + 0.20 × Skills_Gap_norm

Each dimension normalized [0–100]. Affordability weighted highest per ITU Africa evidence showing cost is the primary barrier post-coverage.

Typologies assigned via rule-based classification aligned with ITU's UMC framework. K-means clustering (k=4) confirms typology boundaries.
